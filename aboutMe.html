About Me

Second-Year Software Engineering Student | University of Guelph

I am a second-year Software Engineering student at the University of Guelph with a lifelong passion for computing. While my interest in technology began in childhood, I formally began pursuing this path after high school.

My academic journey includes initial studies at Trent University before transferring to Guelph to better align with my career aspirations in software development. This experience has given me a unique perspective on adapting to new challenges and environments.

Current Focus:

I’m deeply passionate about understanding computing systems at a fundamental level, from hardware architecture to software abstraction layers. Through my studies at the University of Guelph, I’m actively building expertise in:

  • Systems Programming (Memory management)
  • Computational Theory(Algorithms, data structures, complexity analysis)
  • Applied Engineering (Optimization techniques)

    • This academic foundation complements my hands-on projects, creating a feedback loop between theory and practice.
